My Work

  My professional work is mainly dedicated to software development, application of computer algorithms and numerical techniques, and advanced technical applications.
  In 1994 I have started to do research work on optimization of forming processes in C3M, where I have coordinated work on optimization for several years. Within this scope I have developed the optimisation software Inverse.
  Later I have worked in several companies and in diverse fields (e.g. electronics, business applications, geographic information systems, health, material technologies, etc.). My work included research & development, design of different kinds of software (scentific, technical and business), project and team leadership, HR management tasks, etc. Some R&D results are accessible on my document pages.
  I am developing my own base library for development of complex technical and other applications (IGLib), which I have by now used in several applications developed either either in the scope of my employment or within freelance projects.

Software:
  • IGLib.NET (Investigative Generic library) - a base library (utility & framework) for efficient development of technical (especially in the fields of numerics and optimization) and other applications.
  • Optimisatioin shell Inverse, a software for utilisation of various simulatio environments for solution of inverse and optimisation problems; developed within C3M.
  • Optimization library Ioptlib (Investigative optimization library) - a library for development of optimization algorithms, coded in ANSI C.
  • NeuralShell – an industrial-scale software for development of models based on artificial neural networks and for conducting research in the field of ANN-based models.
  • NeuralParametric – a GUI-based application for exploration and analysis of large scale ANN-based models.
  • NeurApp - a lightweight GUI-based application for exploring ANN-based approximation.
  • SInt - A fast versatile interpreter, especially suitable for controlling numerical, for interpreting application initialisation scripts, supporting user routines, etc. conception stage only.
  • ITK - a toolkit based on Tcl/Tk, provides extensions with a few useful utilities.
  • IGS - a simple graphic system for drawing functions and 3D objects.
  • LAF - program for linear approximations, coded in Pascal.
  • A program for Monte Carlo simulation of back scattering of electrons on metallic samples.
