My Work

  My professional work is mainly dedicated to software development, application of computer algorithms and numerical techniques, and advanced engineering applications.
  I have started my R&D career on optimization of forming processes in C3M, where I have coordinated work on optimization in conjunction with finite element-based numerical simulation for several years. Within this scope I developed the optimization software Inverse.
  Later I have worked in several companies and in diverse fields (e.g. electronics, business applications, geographic information systems, health, material technologies, etc.). My work included research & development, design of different kinds of software (scientific, technical and business), project and team leadership, HR management tasks, etc. Some R&D results are accessible on my document pages.
  I have developed my own base library for development of complex technical and other applications (IGLib), which I have used in several applications developed either in the scope of my employment or within freelance projects.

Some Software:
  • IGLib.NET (Investigative Generic library) and related software - a base library (utility & framework) for efficient development of technical (especially in the fields of numerical analysis, optimization, machine vision and artificial intelligence) and other applications.
    • GitHub / Software; Base repository
  • Optimizatioin shell Inverse, a software for utilization of various simulation environments for solution of inverse and optimization problems; developed within C3M.
  • Optimization library Ioptlib (Investigative optimization library) - a library for development of optimization algorithms, coded in ANSI C and C++.
  • NeuralShell – an industrial-scale software for development of models based on artificial neural networks and for conducting research in the field of ANN-based models.
  • AnnApp – a GUI-based application for exploration and analysis of large scale ANN-based models.
  • NeurApp - a lightweight GUI-based application for exploring ANN-based approximation.
  • SInt - A fast versatile interpreter, especially suitable for controlling numerical calculations, for application initialization scripts, supporting user routines and plugins, etc.
  • ITK - a toolkit based on Tcl/Tk, provides extensions with a few useful utilities.
  • IGS - a simple graphic system for drawing functions and 3D objects.
  • LAF - program for linear approximations, coded in Pascal.
  • A program for Monte Carlo simulation of back scattering of electrons on metallic samples.
